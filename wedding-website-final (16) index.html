A Glimpse of Us

Our Story

How It All Began

2022

A Passing Introduction

We assume this is when we met — neither of us can actually remember. What we're pretty confident about is that it would have been a passing introduction the first time we were on worship band together. Clearly, neither made much of an impression. Yet.

2023

A Year Best Forgotten

A horrible year, honestly. We never spoke. We never interacted. Nothing to report here. Moving swiftly on.

2024

The Legendary Sokkie

Then came the sokkie that changed everything. At a charity event at Kelvin Grove, Lia was literally swept off her feet — and the veil was slightly torn in the process. The tannies approved. A sign of things to come.

2025

The Veil, Completely Gone

LIV January arrived and tore the veil completely. One slightly nerve-wracking coffee, a 5-hour Hudson's first date in Green Point, and a Galileo movie night at Kirstenbosch later — Justin and Lia realised what they had been missing all along. Each other.

30 January 2026

Justin made sure they would never be without each other.

And on the 8th of August — it's going to be official.

Praise Jesus! 🙌
